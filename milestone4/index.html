Why Visit Korea?

Korea is a country where ancient traditions meet cutting-edge modernity. From the bustling streets of Seoul to the tranquil temples hidden in the mountains, Korea offers an unforgettable travel experience.

Top Attractions

  • Seoul: Gyeongbokgung Palace, N Seoul Tower, Myeongdong
  • Busan: Haeundae Beach, Gamcheon Culture Village
  • Jeju Island: Hallasan Mountain, Seongsan Ilchulbong
  • DMZ: Panmunjom, Gyeonggi-do Peace observation village

Korean Food Spotlight

  • Kimchi (김치): Fermented and flavorful
  • Bulgogi (불고기): Sweet & savory marinated beef
  • Tteokbokki (떡볶이): Spicy chewy rice cakes
  • Bibimbap (비빔밥): Mixed rice bowl
  • Samgyeopsal (삼겹살): Grilled pork belly with dipping sauces

Learn Korean Basics

Learning a few basic Korean phrases can make your trip more enjoyable. Here are some essentials:

  • Hello: 안녕하세요 (Annyeonghaseyo)
  • Thank you: 감사합니다 (Gamsahamnida)
  • Excuse me / Sorry: 죄송합니다 (Joesonghamnida)
  • How much is this?: 이거 얼마에요? (Igeo eolmaeyo?)
  • Where is the bathroom?: 화장실 어디에요? (Hwajangsil eodieyo?)
  • I don’t understand: 이해하지 못해요 (Ihaehaji mothaeyo)
