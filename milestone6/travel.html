Explore South Korea

Discover the must-visit destinations across Korea, from bustling cities to serene natural wonders.

Seoul

Korea's vibrant capital blending ancient palaces with cutting-edge technology and K-pop culture.

Busan

Coastal city famous for beaches, fresh seafood, and the colorful Gamcheon Culture Village.

Jeju Island

Volcanic island with stunning natural wonders, designated a UNESCO World Natural Heritage site.

Gyeongju

Ancient capital of the Silla Kingdom with countless historical treasures and burial mounds.

DMZ

The Demilitarized Zone offering unique insights into Korea's divided history.

Andong

Center of Korean Confucian culture with well-preserved traditional villages.
